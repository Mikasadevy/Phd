Risk Assessment & Mitigation

The Issue

Rapid degradation of wet samples creates significant biohazard risk during international transport. Lack of immediate autoclave/washing facilities on site.

Strategy

Research will proceed using commercially treated feathers sourced within the UK, ensuring safety compliance and standardized quality.
