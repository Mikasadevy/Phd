üîç Executive Summary

The Lebanese textile sector comprises approximately 800 manufacturing units, representing 14% of the national industrial base. While national aggregate textile waste tonnages are not published, convergent MSW composition evidence allows a bounded national estimate to be presented. This dashboard constructs a scientific profile based on national waste composition data, UNIDO MED TEST II case studies, and industrial intensity mapping.
