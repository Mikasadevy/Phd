The study covers cradle-to-gate boundaries with a firing temperature of 850¬∞C. üéì PhD Context & Relevance

Relevance Verdict: Supporting Reference

This paper provides critical quantitative LCA data which is essential for environmental assessment, even if mechanical data is cited from elsewhere. It is useful for benchmarking the environmental trade-offs of olive pomace incorporation.

Recommended Actions

  • Use GWP values as reference points for environmental impact modeling.
  • Note the counter-intuitive increase in GWP with waste addition.
  • Do not use for mechanical property benchmarking (data absent).

üå± Sustainability Analysis

Key Sustainability Findings

The study reveals a trade-off. While incorporating olive pomace (a waste product) reduces the consumption of virgin clay (improving abiotic depletion), the GWP (Global Warming Potential) actually increases for the specific boundaries studied.

GWP Impact

Increases by 19-63% compared to control.

Resource Use

Reduction in abiotic resource depletion due to clay substitution.
