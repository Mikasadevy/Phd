üå± Sustainability Impact

‚ôªÔ∏è

Waste Valorization

Olive Mill Wastewater contains problematic polyphenols. Incorporating it into bricks immobilizes these compounds within the ceramic matrix, offering an eco-friendly disposal route for the olive oil industry.

Water Conservation

100% substitution of fresh water reduces the water footprint of brick manufacturing in arid regions (like Tunisia).

Industrial Integration

Requires no changes to existing "stiff-mud" industrial processes, ensuring high practical feasibility.
