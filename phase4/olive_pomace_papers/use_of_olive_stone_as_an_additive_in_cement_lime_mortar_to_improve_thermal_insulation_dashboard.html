Use of olive stone as an additive in cement lime mortar to improve thermal insulation

üéì Core Research Contribution ‚úì Extracted

The authors investigated the use of olive stone waste as an additive in cement lime mortar to improve thermal insulation. Specimens were prepared with 0%, 20%, 40%, 50%, 60%, and 70% olive stone addition by dry weight. Thermal conductance, density, and water absorption were measured on 300x300x20mm panels after 28 days of curing.
