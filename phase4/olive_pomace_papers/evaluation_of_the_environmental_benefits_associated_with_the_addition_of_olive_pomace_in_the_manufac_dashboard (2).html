üéì Executive Summary ‚úì Extracted

The authors conducted a Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) using SimaPro and CML 2000 methodology to evaluate the environmental impact of manufacturing lightweight aggregates (LWA) with olive pomace (OP) as a partial clay substitute (1.25, 2.5, and 5 wt%). The study defined the functional unit as 1 kg of aggregate and considered a cradle-to-door scope. Results indicated that the addition of OP reduced environmental impacts, specifically Global Warming Potential, by 3.8% for 1.25 wt% OP, 7.7% for 2.5 wt% OP, and 15.3% for 5 wt% OP compared to the control. The optimal addition range was identified as 1.25 to 2.5 wt% OP based on a balance of environmental benefits and technological properties.
