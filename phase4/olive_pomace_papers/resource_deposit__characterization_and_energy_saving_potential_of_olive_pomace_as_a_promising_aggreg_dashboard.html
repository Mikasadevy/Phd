üéì Core Takeaway ‚ö° Interpretive

Incorporating 40% olive pomace (by mass) into unfired clay bricks reduces thermal conductivity by 55.68% (down to 0.39 W/mK) and density by 17.8%, though it significantly reduces compressive strength. The study validates olive pomace as a viable bio-aggregate for thermal insulation in earth-based construction within circular economy frameworks.
