Actionable Takeaway

This paper establishes a clear 20 wt% upper limit for olive pomace ash in fired clay bricks. For PhD work involving similar biomass ashes, this serves as a baseline for formulation windows (0-20%) and expected property tradeoffs (strength loss vs. insulation gain).
