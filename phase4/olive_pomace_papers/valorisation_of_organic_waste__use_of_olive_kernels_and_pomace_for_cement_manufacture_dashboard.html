Missing Data Points

The extraction identified specific parameters not reported in the text:

  • Durability Properties: Tests other than strength (e.g., chloride, sulfate resistance) were not reported.
  • Cost Analysis: No economic feasibility study included.
  • Leaching Tests: Environmental safety regarding leaching was not tested.

Actionable Takeaway

Use this paper as a benchmark for 10% substitution. It confirms that at low substitution levels (10%), olive waste (whether dried kernels or calcined pomace) does not compromise the 365-day mechanical integrity of the mortar.
