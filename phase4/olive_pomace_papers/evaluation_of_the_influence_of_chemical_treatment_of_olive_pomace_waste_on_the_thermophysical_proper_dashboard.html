üéì PhD Context

Relevance to Sustainable Construction

This paper provides a reference for using agricultural by-products (Olive Pomace) in lightweight insulating concretes. It highlights the potential for chemical activation (NaOH) to modify the interface or absorption properties of lignocellulosic fillers, though the trade-off with thermal conductivity (which seems higher than control at low replacements) needs careful analysis.
