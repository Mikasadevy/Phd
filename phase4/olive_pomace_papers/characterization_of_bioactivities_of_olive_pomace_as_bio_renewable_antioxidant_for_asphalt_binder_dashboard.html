üéì Core Research Contribution ‚ö° Interpretive

The study demonstrates that Processed Olive Pomace (POP) acts as an effective antioxidant in Asphalt Binder (PG 64-22). At an optimal dosage of 15% by weight , it significantly retards thermal oxidative aging (measured by Carbonyl Index growth) and improves binder-aggregate adhesion (Vialit test). However, its effectiveness is reduced under natural weather aging due to UV exposure.
