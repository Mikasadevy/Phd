‚ö†Ô∏è Limitations & Risks

Compatibility Issues

OWB is acidic (pH 4-5). OWS in cement mixtures showed a "lack of adhesion during the cement hydration process," leading to its exclusion from cement blocks and reassignment to particleboards.

Performance Drop-off

High waste content (20% OWB in blocks) causes significant strength reduction, failing standard compliance.

Workability

Larger amounts of ash (OWA) made mortar workability difficult, requiring adjustments.

Data Gaps

Durability tests are mentioned as "ongoing/future" and not included in this dataset. Numeric strength tables are missing (only figures provided).

  • ‚úì Processing Energy: Wet bagasse was used as a paste without energy-intensive drying, though acidity must be managed.
