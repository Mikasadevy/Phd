üéì Core Research Contribution ‚ö° Interpretive

The study demonstrates that ground Olive Pomace Ash (OPA) can serve as a partial cement substitute in mortars. While replacements up to 50% were tested, the optimal threshold for maintaining acceptable structural integrity and durability is 20%. Beyond this level, significant drops in compressive strength and freeze-thaw resistance were observed.
