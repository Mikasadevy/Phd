üéì Core Research Finding ‚úì Modeled

The study models the environmental performance of incorporating Olive Mill Wastewater (OMWW) (replacing 100% mixing water) and Waste Pomace (WP) (5% mass addition) into ceramic bricks. Results indicate a 37% GWP reduction for WP and 8% reduction for OMWW compared to conventional bricks. Transport distance sensitivity is identified as a critical constraint (Break-even: 641 km for OMWW).
