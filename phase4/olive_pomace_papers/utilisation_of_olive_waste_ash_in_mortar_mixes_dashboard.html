üéì Actionable Takeaway for PhD ‚ö° Interpretive

Olive waste ash can replace up to 15% of silica sand in mortar to improve compressive strength (from 26.5 to 38.2 MPa), but it reduces workability and setting time. This dataset is valuable for modeling compressive strength trade-offs and benchmarking agricultural ash performance as a fine aggregate replacement.
