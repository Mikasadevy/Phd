"Central Composite Design optimization identified a self-compacting mortar mix with 6.66% olive pomace bottom ash replacement achieving 48.66 MPa compressive strength."

Strict Summary: The authors incinerated olive pomace at 600¬∞C to produce ash (OPBA), which was ground to <60 ¬µm. They used a Central Composite Design (CCD) to test self-compacting mortars with 0-20% OPBA replacement, 10-20% limestone filler, and 0.4-0.5 W/B ratio. Increasing OPBA decreased slump and strength while increasing water absorption. An optimal mix (6.66% OPBA, 20% filler, 0.42 W/B) achieved 48.66 MPa compressive strength and 26.3 cm slump.
