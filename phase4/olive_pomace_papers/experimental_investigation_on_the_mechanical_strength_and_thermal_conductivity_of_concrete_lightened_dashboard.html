üéì Core Research Takeaway ‚ö° Interpretive

The study demonstrates that replacing natural sand with 5% Olive Pomace Aggregates (OPA) yields a structural lightweight concrete with 25 MPa compressive strength and significantly improved thermal insulation (0.86 W/mK), representing a 34% reduction in conductivity compared to the control. However, replacement levels above 5% lead to substantial strength degradation.
