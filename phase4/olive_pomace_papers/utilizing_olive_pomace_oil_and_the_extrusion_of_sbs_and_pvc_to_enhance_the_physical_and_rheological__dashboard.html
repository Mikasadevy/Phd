Primary Objective

‚úì Extracted

Evaluate the efficacy of olive pomace oil (OPO) and polymer blend, created with polyvinyl chloride (PVC) and styrene-butadiene-styrene (SBS), in modifying the properties of asphalt binder.

Technical Problem

Bio-oils improve low-temperature performance but diminish high-temperature performance (rutting resistance).

Environmental Problem

Depletion of petroleum resources and disposal of olive pomace waste causing pollution.
