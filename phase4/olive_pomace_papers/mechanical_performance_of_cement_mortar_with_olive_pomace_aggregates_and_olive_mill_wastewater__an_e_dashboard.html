üéì Core Research Contribution ‚ö° Interpretive

The study substitutes natural sand with Olive Pomace Aggregates (OPA) and mixes with Olive Mill Wastewater (OMW). It establishes that a 5% substitution of dry OPA/OMW yields a viable lightened mortar (33.5 MPa at 28 days), while higher replacements (10-15%) significantly degrade workability and strength. The dataset is complete for workability trends and optimal mix performance.
