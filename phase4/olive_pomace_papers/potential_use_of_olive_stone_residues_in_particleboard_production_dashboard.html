Olive stone residues (OSR) are viable in particleboard at 10% substitution without pre-treatment. Higher loadings (>10%) degrade mechanical strength despite improving thickness swelling and formaldehyde emissions. Alkali pretreatment improves strength for high-volume replacement but negatively impacts swelling.
