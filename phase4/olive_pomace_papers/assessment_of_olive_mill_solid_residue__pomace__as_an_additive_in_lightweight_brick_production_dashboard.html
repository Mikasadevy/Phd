üéØ Research Objective & Problem Statement

Primary Objective

‚úì Extracted

"Evaluate the possibility of obtaining a lightweight construction material with good insulating characteristics using wet pomace as a pore-forming additive."

Problem Definition

‚úì Extracted

Environmental Problem

Disposal of olive oil industrial waste (wet pomace) which is polluting and difficult to treat. It cannot be composted or burned without expensive pre-treatment.

Proposed Solution

Incorporate wet pomace directly into clay brick matrices to burn off during firing, creating pores (insulation) and consuming the waste.
