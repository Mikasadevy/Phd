This study establishes the viability of using Olive Pomace Waste (80% by weight) combined with polymer fibers to manufacture thermal insulation panels. It contrasts two manufacturing routes: Hot-Press Moulding and Continuous Lamination. The findings provide a comprehensive dataset on density, thermal conductivity, and thermal resistance for bio-composite insulators.
