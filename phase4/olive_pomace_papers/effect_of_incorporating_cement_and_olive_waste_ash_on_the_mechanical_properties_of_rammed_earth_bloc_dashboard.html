Effect of Incorporating Cement and Olive Waste Ash on the Mechanical Properties of Rammed Earth Block

üéì Core Research Contribution ‚úì Extracted

This study investigates the use of Olive Waste Ash (OWA) as a partial soil replacement and cement as a stabilizer to improve the mechanical characteristics of rammed earth blocks. The findings demonstrate that OWA exhibits pozzolanic behavior, significantly improving compressive strength (UCS) at replacements up to 30%, despite a reduction in density.
