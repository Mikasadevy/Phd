üéì Core Research Takeaway ‚úì Extracted

Partial replacement of cement with olive waste ash (OWA) up to 15% reduces compressive strength and density while increasing setting time and reducing soundness expansion. The study highlights the trade-off between environmental conservation (using waste) and mechanical performance reduction in cement pastes.
