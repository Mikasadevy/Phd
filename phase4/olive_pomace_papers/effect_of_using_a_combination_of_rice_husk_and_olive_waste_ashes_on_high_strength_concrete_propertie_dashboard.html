Mechanical Performance Comparison (28 Days)

Microstructure

SEM analysis revealed that RHA and OWA interact with CH to produce additional C-S-H gel (pozzolanic reaction) and act as fillers, creating a denser, more compact microstructure with fewer voids.

Workability Trade-off

Slump values exhibited a decreasing trend with increasing ash content due to the high surface area and water demand of the fine ash particles.
